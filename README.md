## Intelligent Revenue Forecasting & Customer Lifecycle Analytics (Salesforce)

### Overview

This project is an end-to-end Salesforce implementation that simulates how a modern subscription-based company manages its revenue pipeline and customer lifecycle.

Instead of treating CRM as just a record-keeping tool, this system focuses on **decision-making and analytics**, combining sales data, customer usage patterns, and predictive scoring to provide actionable insights.

---

### What This Project Does

The system models a complete business flow:

**Lead → Opportunity → Subscription → Product Usage → Customer Health → Revenue Insights**

It automates key operations like:
* Lead qualification using scoring logic
* Opportunity success prediction
* Subscription lifecycle tracking
* Customer usage monitoring
* Churn risk estimation
* Revenue forecasting and reporting

---

### Unique Approach

What makes this project different is the introduction of a **Lifecycle Intelligence layer**.

Instead of analyzing data in isolation, the system combines:
* Lead quality
* Deal probability
* Product engagement
* Customer satisfaction

These are merged into a single **Lifecycle Score**, which helps classify customers into categories like:
* High Value
* Growth Potential
* At Risk
* High Churn Risk

This provides a more holistic view of customer behavior and revenue stability, going beyond standard CRM implementations.
